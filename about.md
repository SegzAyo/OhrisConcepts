# Ohris Concepts

## Building intelligent software that solves real problems

### Who we are

Ohris Concepts is a technology company building a family of software products that make everyday life simpler and help people and businesses make better decisions.

We're guided by one belief: technology should feel intelligent, intuitive, and genuinely useful. Every product we ship blends thoughtful design, solid engineering, and practical AI to solve problems that actually matter.

We aren't tied to a single industry. Ohris Concepts is the parent company behind a growing portfolio spanning financial technology, fashion technology, productivity, and applied AI — with more on the way.

Our goal is simple: **build software people genuinely love to use.**

## Our products

### Xpensely — your money, understood

An AI-powered personal finance platform that helps people understand, manage, and grow their money.

At its core is **FinBot**, a voice-enabled financial assistant that goes well beyond expense tracking:

- Intelligent insights into where your money actually goes
- Personalized planning and goal setting
- Budgeting and spending analysis
- Multi-account management
- Natural, conversational financial guidance

**The vision:** make quality financial advice accessible to everyone.

### Modiq — your wardrobe, reimagined

An AI-powered fashion platform that helps people get more out of the clothes they already own.

Instead of pushing you to buy more, Modiq curates outfits from your existing wardrobe — then suggests the few pieces genuinely worth adding to complete your style.

- AI outfit curation from your own closet
- A digital wardrobe you can browse anywhere
- Occasion-based styling and recommendations
- Smart, intentional shopping suggestions
- Everyday fashion inspiration

## How we build

Great products start with people, not technology. Our process is a loop, not a line:

1. **Understand** — research the real problem.
2. **Design** — craft an intuitive experience.
3. **Build** — engineer reliable, scalable software.
4. **Learn** — improve through feedback and product analytics.
5. **Evolve** — use AI to make the product smarter over time.
